Deceptive Chart

"More Gaming = Better Grades!"

← Back to A10

Figure 1: Exam scores show dramatic improvement as gaming hours increase, suggesting a strong causal relationship between video game play and academic success.

Manipulation Techniques Used:

  1. Truncated Y-Axis: The y-axis starts at 75 instead of 0, making a 15-point increase (78→93) appear as a near-vertical climb, exaggerating the effect.
  2. Exaggerated Aspect Ratio: The chart is tall and narrow (500×400), which visually steepens the slope and makes the trend appear more dramatic than it actually is.
