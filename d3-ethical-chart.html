Gaming Hours vs Exam Scores (Honest Representation)

← Back to A10

Figure 2: Exam scores for one student over 7 weeks. While there is a correlation between gaming hours and scores, this single-student sample cannot establish causation. Other factors like study time, exam difficulty, and sleep were not controlled.

Ethical Design Choices:

  1. Y-Axis Starts at 0: Shows the true proportion of change (78→93 is only a 15-point increase out of 100).
  2. Proportional Aspect Ratio: Standard 600×400 ratio does not exaggerate the slope.
  3. Neutral Color: Uses blue instead of green to avoid implying "good" or "success."
  4. Honest Caption: Acknowledges limitations and does not claim causation.
